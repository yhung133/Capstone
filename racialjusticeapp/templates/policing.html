Policing Visualization Tools Introduction

The visualization tools focus on characteristics of the arrested population in Champaign and Urbana from January 2010 to October 2016. Data for these tools come from the police departments in Champaign, Urbana, and the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Arrests include police incidents in which an individual is charged with a violation of the law. This means the arrest data includes data for individuals who were issued citations and traffic tickets, along with individuals who were charged with more serious crimes.

Instructions For Users

Use the tabs on the left to navigate between tools that explore different aspects of the data. You can use the tools in each tab to compare the racial breakdown of arrested individuals based on a number of variables, such as crime type, age of arrestee, etc.
